# Bayra Travel Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 04, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Bayra Travel (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Passenger and Driver mobile applications (the "App") and our services in Arba Minch, Ethiopia.

## 2. Information We Collect
*   **Personal Information:** When you register, we collect your Name, Phone Number, and Email Address.
*   **Location Data:** 
    *   **Passengers:** We collect your precise location data to allow you to set pickup and destination points.
    *   **Drivers:** We collect precise location data **even when the app is in the background or closed** to enable ride matching and allow passengers to track their approaching ride.
*   **Payment Data:** We process payments through secure online payment integrations. We do not store your full bank details on our servers; we only store transaction references for bookkeeping.
*   **Device Data:** We collect device tokens (FCM Tokens) to send you real-time push notifications regarding your ride status.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
*   To facilitate and manage rides between Passengers and Drivers.
*   To calculate accurate fares based on distance and logic.
*   To provide real-time updates and notifications.
*   To maintain the Dual-Vault Ledger for bookkeeping.
*   To ensure the safety and security of the Bayra community.

## 4. Background Location (Critical for Drivers)
The Bayra Driver App collects location data to enable real-time ride tracking and dispatching **even when the app is closed or not in use.** This is a core requirement of the service. Drivers must grant "Allow all the time" location permission for the App to function.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure
*   **Between Users:** We share the Passenger’s name and pickup location with the Driver, and the Driver’s name and location with the Passenger.
*   **Service Providers:** We share data with Google Firebase and payment gateways only as necessary to provide services.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion
You may request the deletion of your account and personal data at any time by contacting us at **bayratravel@gmail.com**.
