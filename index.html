Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 07, 2026

Bayra Travel is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Data Collection

We collect your Name, Phone Number, Email, and Precise Location to provide ride-hailing services, driver dispatching, and security monitoring.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used solely to facilitate ride-matching, ensure driver safety, and process payments. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Security

All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols.

Account & Data Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to bayratravel@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".
  3. Include your Name and Phone Number.

Your data will be permanently erased from our Firebase database within 7 business days.
