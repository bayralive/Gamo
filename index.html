Account & Data Deletion Request

At Bayra Travel, your privacy is our priority. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from our servers, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to bayratravel@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".
  3. Include the Name and Phone Number registered to your account.

What happens next?

  • Data Deleted: Your Name, Phone Number, Email, FCM Tokens, and entire Ride History will be permanently erased from our active Firebase database.
  • Retention: The deletion process will be completed within 7 business days of your request. No personal data is retained after this period.
